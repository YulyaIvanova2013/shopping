Shopping and shopping centres

Task 2
Hello! It’s the electronic assistant of Teenage Magazine online. We kindly ask you to take part in our survey. We would like to find out how teenagers feel about shopping and shopping centres. Please answer six questions. The survey is anonymous – you don’t have to give your name. So, let’s get started.
  1. Who usually does the shopping in your family?
  2. What can you buy in your nearest shopping centre?
  3. How often do you usually go shopping?
  4. Why are shopping centres so popular nowadays?
  5. Why do some people hate shopping?
  6. What time would you recommend for busy people should go shopping?
